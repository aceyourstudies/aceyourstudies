The Process

Simple. Intelligent. Effective.

Three steps stand between you and a perfectly structured study session.

01

Upload Your PDF

Send us any PDF study material — lecture notes, textbook chapters, revision guides, or past papers. We accept all standard academic formats.

02

Intelligent Chunking

Our automated system analyses your document, identifies logical study blocks, extracts key concepts, and structures the content into digestible, quiz-ready segments.

03

Receive Your Google Form

A custom Google Form quiz built directly from your material is sent straight to you. Open it anywhere, share with classmates, and start practising immediately.
